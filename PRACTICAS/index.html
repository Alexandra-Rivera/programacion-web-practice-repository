Paris: The City of Love

All people agree that Paris is called as the city of love and romance. Every couple in the world dreams to spend some time in the city even only once in their lifetime.Even it is much chosen as the place for the honeymoon or for engagement with the unforgettable moment.There are many reasons that can make the city as the city of love and romance.

Why is it called that way?

There are several reasons for that, here you have some:

Eiffel Tower

Eiffel Tower is not only a common city icon but also becomes a symbol of love for many couples from all over the world. It is because of thousands of marriage proposal made under the beautiful tower every year. Eiffel Tower has every unique element; the strong iron tower with the amazing design, the 20 thousand of twinkling lights in every five minutes creating the romantic atmosphere, the beautiful view of the city from the tower top, and the wonderfully designed landscape.

The City With Romantic Lights Every Night

Paris is always illuminated by the romantic lights every night. The beautiful light is illuminating the beautiful landscapes where the lovers enjoy the night including on the boats above the Seine after sunset. It is a romantic atmosphere to enjoy while having some dinner. No other city that offers the same beauty and romance of Paris with the special lighting in many places.

Delicious Food and Wine

The delicious food and wine of French are also the great reasons of why many people love the city. Paris is the place of good food with sophisticated tastes. Every couple who visit the city must try the good tasted foods and wine for a romantic dinner that they will never forget.

Many Romantic Places to Say Love

Yes, Paris provides many romantic places to say love to your beloved one. For example, on the Wall of I Love You/ Mur de je t’aime on Montmartre Hill. The natural hill of Paris is always visited by romantic couples and they have written I Love You in 311 different languages. A romantic couple will not feel complete before they visit the hill and write on the wall the words I Love You in their language.
